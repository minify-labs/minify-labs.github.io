Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28
App name: MinifyPDF Offline (the "App")
Developer: Juli Kumari ("we", "us")
Contact: minify.labs.app@gmail.com

Summary

MinifyPDF Offline compresses PDF files on your device. We do not upload your PDF files to our servers. The App may display ads using Google AdMob and offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove ads.

Information the App processes

PDF files you choose

  • The App processes PDF files only after you select them using Android's file picker.
  • Compression is performed locally on your device.
  • We do not access your files in the background, and we do not upload your PDFs.

Output files

  • The compressed PDF is saved to the location you choose in the save dialog.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide and measure ads, such as device identifiers, approximate location, app activity, and diagnostic information. This information is processed by Google according to Google's policies. We do not receive your PDF contents from AdMob.

In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App offers an optional one-time purchase ("Remove Ads (Lifetime)"). Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Google (AdMob) for advertising and measurement, if ads are enabled.
  • Google Play for processing in-app purchases.

Data retention

  • We do not store your PDF files on our servers because we do not upload them.
  • Any PDFs and compressed outputs remain on your device and can be deleted by you.

Security

We aim to use industry-standard security practices. Network communications performed by third-party services (such as AdMob) are encrypted in transit.

Children's privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information via third-party ads, please contact us.

Your choices

  • You can use the App offline. Ads may not load without an internet connection.
  • You can remove ads permanently by purchasing "Remove Ads (Lifetime)".
  • You can reset the device Advertising ID in your phone settings (if available).

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the Effective date above when changes are made.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: minify.labs.app@gmail.com.
